1. Executive Summary

The world is entering a resource crisis driven by the exponential growth of AI, electrification, and cloud infrastructure. AI data centers are consuming enormous volumes of electricity and water, placing severe strain on local grids and municipal systems, while simultaneously generating the richest gold-bearing electronic waste stream in history.

At the same time, North America contains hundreds of millions of tons of unprocessed mine waste, much of it sitting on the surface in abandoned or inactive mining districts. Recovering metals from these “stranded assets” is economically and environmentally impossible using conventional means.

The core failure across both sectors is identical:

Traditional extraction—whether in mining or e-waste recycling—is too toxic, too centralized, too energy-intensive, and too slow to permit.

Minetek + Breakthrough Management together solve this crisis through a modular, zero-waste, mobile extraction platform that can recover gold and critical metals directly at the source—with no tailings ponds, no liquid discharge, and dramatically reduced permitting risk.

This unified PDD serves as the foundational document guiding the development of a new digital presence that explains this opportunity and differentiator clearly for data centers, landowners, and investors.

2. The Problem (The “Why” Driving This Project)

The website must clarify and visualize three interconnected global problems:

A. The AI Infrastructure Crisis (“Dirty Infrastructure Paradox”)
Context

The AI and cloud economy is creating unprecedented demand for compute infrastructure. Each hyperscale data center consumes:

Up to 2 MW of continuous power (equal to ~2,000 homes)

Millions of gallons of water annually for cooling

Significant volumes of high-value server e-waste containing gold, silver, palladium, copper, and rare materials

These facilities frequently cause utility rate increases, accelerated depreciation of local infrastructure, and environmental strain.

Pain Point

Hyperscalers must demonstrate high-integrity ESG compliance.
They cannot rely on traditional recyclers due to:

Hazardous chemicals

Inconsistent recovery rates

Weak provenance documentation

Lack of traceability

Carbon-intensive transport

Poorly documented gold can incur penalties up to 20% below spot price, making traceability a financial necessity.

Website Requirement

Position Minetek as:

“The Cleanest Link in the AI Materials Supply Chain.”

The site must visually demonstrate:

Zero Liquid Discharge

On-site processing

Material traceability

Closed-loop circularity (Server → Recovery → Reuse)

B. The Abandoned Mine Crisis (“Stranded Asset Dilemma”)
Context

Across the U.S. are 28,000+ abandoned mine sites. Many contain vast piles of surface ore (“dump ore”) with meaningful gold values. Colorado alone contains 100+ million tons of such material. However:

Traditional mills require tailings ponds, cyanide, acid leaching, and high water usage

Permitting new mills is nearly impossible

Landowners face environmental liability

Transportation costs are prohibitive

Thus, waste rock becomes a liability, not an asset.

Pain Point

Landowners cannot monetize their resource without incurring environmental, legal, and financial risks.

Website Requirement

Demonstrate Breakthrough Management’s ability to create a permitted, compliant pathway—supported by the successful Gladstone Mill approval, the first such approval in 20 years.

C. The Logistics & Capital Gap
Context

Both industries face the same limitation:

Transporting material to centralized facilities is costly

Fixed infrastructure requires high CAPEX

Small and mid-sized operators cannot access processing solutions

ESG pressure opposes long-haul logistics

Pain Point

Traditional extraction models depend on massive centralized facilities that no longer make economic or environmental sense.

Website Requirement

Visualize Minetek’s solution:

“We bring the mill to the material.”

Display the 7-trailer MRU system and communicate:

Mobility

Low permitting footprint

Lower energy use

No toxic outputs

Immediate on-site value creation

3. Market Opportunity

The market opportunity for Minetek + Breakthrough exists at the intersection of two under-served, rapidly expanding reserves:

A. Urban Ore (E-Waste from AI Growth)

Global e-waste: 62M tons/year, growing rapidly

Server PCBs: 40–800× more gold per ton than mined ore

Only 22% formally recycled today

Urban Ore is now the highest-grade gold reserve on Earth but cannot be cleanly processed at scale via traditional methods.

B. Legacy Ore (Dump Piles & Abandoned Mines)

U.S. abandoned mines: 28,000+

Colorado surface ore: >100M tons

Many sites contain economically significant gold

Virtually all are stranded due to permitting and environmental constraints

Dump ore is a multi-billion-dollar opportunity trapped behind regulatory barriers.

C. Revenue Trajectory

MineTeck projects scaling revenue:
$7M → $34M from 2025–2027 through combined Urban Ore + Legacy Ore operations in Colorado.
