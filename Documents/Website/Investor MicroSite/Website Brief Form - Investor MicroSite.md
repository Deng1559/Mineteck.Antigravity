## **2. BUSINESS CONTEXT**

### **Organization Background**

The Investor MicroSite is the primary engagement point for accredited investors participating in Breakthrough Management Gold Fund I. It provides a secure environment to access the investment thesis, portfolio details, and technical documentation.

**Key Features:**

- **Accreditation Gate:** Mandatory verification for non-public data.
- **Unified Portfolio View:** Integrated data from MineTeck and Urban Mining.
- **Data Room:** Secure repository for financial models, offtake agreements, and legal docs.
