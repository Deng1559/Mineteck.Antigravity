## **2\. BUSINESS CONTEXT**

### **Organization Background**

Urban Mining LLC is a strategic portfolio company within Breakthrough Management Gold Fund I, specializing in environmentally responsible precious metal recovery from electronic waste (e-waste). Operating out of EWC Group Inc.'s 14,000 sq ft facility in Kent, WA, the company represents a critical component of the Fund's "clean mining" investment thesis.

**Key Differentiators:**

* Partnership with IMG Gold Recovery Inc. (proprietary reagent-free extraction)  
* Secured offtake agreement with Ocean Partners (global metals broker)  
* EPA-validated concentration rates: 40-800x more gold than mined ore  
* Zero-cyanide, zero-mercury processing technology  
* Commercial-scale capacity: 300 lbs PCBs/hour, 600 tons annually

### **Strategic Position**

This microsite serves the dual purpose of:

1. **Investor Education** \- Demonstrating ESG-aligned urban mining as complement to traditional mining operations  
2. **Industry Credibility** \- Showcasing technical capability and commercial viability to attract B2B partnerships

### **Platform/CMS**

**Recommendation:** Next.js 14 \+ Headless CMS (Sanity or Contentful)

**Rationale:**

* **SEO-Critical:** Static generation for technical content, investor searches  
* **Performance:** Sub-2-second load times (investor credibility factor)  
* **Security:** Gated content, investor portal integration capability  
* **Scalability:** Easy to expand to full Breakthrough ecosystem microsites

* Trade-off: Less flexible for future CRM/portal integration

## **16\. RISKS & MITIGATION**

### **Technical Risks**

**Risk:** Data room security breach exposing investor financials  
 **Mitigation:** Penetration testing, SOC 2 compliant hosting, IP whitelisting option, activity logging

**Risk:** CRM integration failure causing lead loss  
 **Mitigation:** Fallback email notifications, daily sync verification, duplicate prevention logic

### **Compliance Risks**

**Risk:** Inadvertent general solicitation violating Reg D 506(c)  
 **Mitigation:** Legal pre-launch review, accreditation verification before financial access, disclaimer on every page

**Risk:** Environmental claims challenged as greenwashing  
 **Mitigation:** Cite only third-party validated data, include methodology footnotes, avoid subjective language ("world's best")

### **Business Risks**

**Risk:** Low investor conversion despite traffic  
 **Mitigation:** A/B testing program, exit-intent surveys, sales team qualitative feedback loop

**Risk:** Supply partner skepticism due to nascent operation  
 **Mitigation:** Prioritize IMG partnership credibility, offer pilot program (low-risk test shipment), facility tour invitations
