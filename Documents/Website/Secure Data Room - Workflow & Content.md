# **SECURE DATA ROOM & NDA WORKFLOW**

## **1. OVERVIEW**

The Secure Data Room is the final stage of the investor journey. It is a strictly controlled environment containing sensitive financial, technical, and legal documentation required for final investment diligence.

---

## **2. GATEWAY: THE ACCREDITATION & NDA FLOW**

**Step 1: The Questionnaire (HubSpot Form)**

- Name, Entity, Jurisdiction.
- Verification of Accredited Status (Reg D 506(c)).
- Confirmation of investment interest level.

**Step 2: NDA Execution (DocuSign/PandaDoc)**

- Automated send of the Breakthrough Management Mutual NDA upon form completion.
- Signature tracking triggers the next step.

**Step 3: Access Granted**

- Investor receives a unique, trackable link to the Supabase-backed document portal.

## **5. SECURITY & COMPLIANCE RULES**

- **Watermarking:** All downloaded PDFs are dynamically watermarked with the investor's email and timestamp.
- **Activity Tracking:** Management receives alerts when specific folders/financial models are accessed.
- **IP Whitelisting:** (Optional for Institutional) Access restricted to specific IPs.
- **No Public Indexing:** `robots.txt` set to block all search engine crawlers.
